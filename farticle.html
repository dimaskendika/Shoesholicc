NH X COMPASS . VELOCITY COMPASS® / NEIGHBORHOOD®

COMPASS® proudly comes together with the revered Japanese brand NEIGHBORHOOD for the exclusive release of their limited-edition sneaker collaboration, The NH X COMPASS . VELOCITY.

Established in 1998 by Kahar Gunawan in Bandung, Indonesia, COMPASS® has carried a strong legacy of shoemaking craft that directs its focus on local artistry and quality. Following the brand's rebranding in 2018 with Aji Handoko Purbo at the helm as creative director, the brand has seen a streak of successful collaborations with both local and international brands, including Japanese brand Fxxking Rabbits and psychedelic music project Tame Impala.

NEIGHBORHOOD®, on the other hand, is a trailblazer in Japanese streetwear. Founded by Shinsuke Takizawa in 1994, the brand has grown out of its first brick-and-mortar store in the back streets of Harajuku to receiving international acclaim, as it continues to roll out coveted fashion pieces heavily influenced by Japanese subcultures, tagged with edgy designs and a distinctly irreverent spirit.

This exciting collaboration between COMPASS® and NEIGHBORHOOD® marks a significant milestone for the Indonesian brand as it extends its reach into the Ura Harajuku circle.
